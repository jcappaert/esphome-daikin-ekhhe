# Hardware

This page will document the physical wiring and installation notes for connecting an ESPHome device to the Daikin EKHHE / Altherma M HW RS485 bus.

The short version:

- Keep the original display connected.
- Tap the RS485 bus rather than replacing the display.
- Connect the ESP32 UART through an RS485 transceiver to `A`, `B`, and `GND` on the display bus.
- Verify all connector pinouts on your unit before powering anything.

More detailed wiring notes will be migrated here from the README.
